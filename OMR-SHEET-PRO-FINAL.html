🔒 Is my data safe?

All your exam data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded unless you use the Backup feature.

📱 Does it work offline?

Yes! Once loaded, OMR SHEET PRO works fully offline. No internet needed during exams.

📄 How do I save results as PDF?

After submitting an exam, tap the "Save PDF" button in the Result screen.

📚 What is the Library?

Save any question during or after an exam for quick revision later. Access from the Home screen.

🗂 What are Exam Sets?

Pre-configure an exam (questions, types, options, negative marking) and save it. Reuse it anytime without reconfiguring.
